## **Solution Overview**

**XinBridge 心桥** is an AI-powered emotional support app designed for cancer patients and their families. It provides companionship during challenging times through cloud-based Gentle Companion conversations, mood tracking, a Night Sanctuary feature for moments of need, and tools to strengthen family connections.
