AuroWeaver Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

Introduction

Welcome to AuroWeaver. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI workflow platform.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

We may collect several types of information from and about users of our platform, including:

  • Account information such as username, email address, and password that you provide when registering.
  • Workflow data, process definitions, execution logs, and other content you create or run within AuroWeaver.
  • Information about your device, including IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Usage data such as features used, execution history, and platform interaction statistics.
  • Integration credentials (e.g., GitHub, Slack, Email) that you configure—stored encrypted.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the AuroWeaver platform.
  • Execute workflows and processes on your behalf.
  • Store and manage your workflows, processes, and execution history.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Send technical notices, updates, and administrative messages.
  • Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access.

Local LLM and Data Privacy

AuroWeaver supports local LLM options (Ollama, Hugging Face, LM Studio) that run entirely on your infrastructure. When you use local LLM, no data is sent to external AI services. When you use cloud AI providers (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini), data is processed according to their respective privacy policies.

Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose personal information that we collect or you provide:

  • To comply with any court order, law, or legal process.
  • To enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered into between you and us.
  • If we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of our company, our users, or others.

Data Security

We have implemented measures designed to secure your personal information from accidental loss and from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure. Credentials for integrations are encrypted. However, we cannot guarantee that unauthorized third parties will never be able to defeat those measures.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we have collected about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@aurozeon.org
