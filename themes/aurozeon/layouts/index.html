See active federal perimeters on a map, search any address, and get a transparent 0–100% screening score combining regional hazard and proximity to incidents—built for awareness, not insurance certification.

AI Chat & Image Analysis

Ask about emergency kits, defensible space, and evacuation concepts. Upload photos of your surroundings for AI vision feedback on potential hazards—always follow local authorities in a real emergency.
