Our Products

ByeBites: Craving Control App

ByeBites helps users take control of their eating habits with practical fasting timers, craving tracking, and motivational insights. Designed for busy adults and families, the app promotes mindful eating without requiring complex routines or strict diets.

Energy Tracker Tools

We develop simple web dashboards and mobile tools to help households and local governments better understand energy usage, compare trends, and find ways to reduce waste. Our tools are built with clarity and action in mind, not complexity.

Custom Tools for Public Good

We build specialized mobile and web apps that make data more accessible and help people take informed action. Each product is crafted to solve a real-world problem with simplicity and purpose.
