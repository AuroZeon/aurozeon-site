Wildfire Assistant

Know your risk. Plan ahead. Stay informed.

AI-powered wildfire risk assessment and preparedness. NIFC perimeter maps, address-based risk scoring (0–100%), GPT-4 chat for emergency guidance, and AI vision analysis of your surroundings—built with Vite, React, and Capacitor.

Built for Life Safety

Screening-level awareness and education—not a replacement for official NFPA, USFS, FEMA, or local authority assessments.

Live Map & NIFC Data

Current wildfire perimeters from federal WFIGS data (NIFC), animated markers, distance-aware context. Cached for performance.

Address Risk Score

0.0–1.0 score (shown as %): regional hazard prior, proximity to active incidents, containment stress—transparent methodology.

AI Emergency Chat

Continuous conversation with context memory. Preparedness tips, evacuation concepts, and professional wildfire guidance.

Image Analysis

Upload photos of your property; AI vision highlights hazards, combustibles, and defensible-space style recommendations.

More Features

Everything you need in one app.

Geocoding & Search

Google Maps address search and tap-to-place—your coordinates power the risk model.

Chat History

Multiple sessions and saved conversations locally—pick up where you left off.

Vegetation Index

Relative proxy for fuels—useful for comparison within the app, not a forestry inventory.

Native & Web

Vite + React + Capacitor for iOS and Android. Optional FastAPI backend for advanced deployments.
