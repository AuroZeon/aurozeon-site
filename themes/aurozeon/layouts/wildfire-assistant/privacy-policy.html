Wildfire Assistant Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Wildfire Assistant. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile and web application for wildfire risk awareness and preparedness.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we or our service providers may process:

  • Location and address data: When you search for an address or use the map, your queries may be sent to Google Maps (Geocoding, Places, Maps JavaScript API) to obtain coordinates and display maps. Google’s privacy policy applies to that processing.
  • Chat messages: Text you send to the AI assistant may be processed by OpenAI to generate responses. OpenAI’s terms and privacy policy apply.
  • Images: Photos you upload for analysis may be processed by OpenAI vision models. Do not upload images containing sensitive personal information you do not wish to share.
  • Local data on your device: Chat history and session data may be stored in local storage on your device for continuity between sessions.
  • Wildfire data: The app fetches public wildfire perimeter data (e.g., NIFC WFIGS) from third-party sources; this is not personal data about you.
  • Device and usage: Standard mobile identifiers, IP address, and app usage may be collected by app stores (Apple, Google) or analytics as described in their policies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information to:

  • Provide risk scores, maps, AI chat, and image analysis features.
  • Improve reliability and fix technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Third-Party Services

The app relies on third parties including OpenAI, Google Maps Platform, and public wildfire data providers. Their collection and use of data are governed by their respective policies.

Disclosure

We may disclose information where required by law, to protect rights and safety, or as part of a business transfer. We do not sell your personal information.

Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect information. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. Contact us using the information below.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly.
