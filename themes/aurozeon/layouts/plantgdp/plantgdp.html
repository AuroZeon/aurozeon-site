Plant GDP

Your space, your week—plain local shopping tips.

Point your camera at a room, desk, or garden. Plant GDP turns the scene into practical grocery tips, nearby store activity, and suggested next steps for your area. The plant in the frame is context—the value is what it means for how you shop and spend.

How It Works

Camera-first, on-device history, optional location for weather and weekly ads near you.

Scan or Gallery

Allow camera access or pick a photo. Tap Scan and wait a few seconds for price tips, store activity, and next steps.

Scene Context

On-device analysis reads your space and adapts tips to your chosen occupation—office, creator, homemaker, or medical.

Local Stores & Deals

Optional location powers weather lines and weekly-ad data from public retail APIs. Open maps or store sites only after you confirm.

History on Device

Past scans stay in History unless you delete them. Export or share reports from your device when you choose.

Built for Everyday Shopping

Groceries, local stores, and small ways to save time and money—without pretending to be financial advice.

Weather Context

Open-Meteo supplies garden and area weather when you allow location—or you can scan without it.

Weekly Ads

Flipp-powered flyer data helps surface verified product deals when postal code and store context are available.

Safe External Links

Maps, store websites, and deal searches open only after an in-app confirmation—we never silently leave the app.

Native iOS & Android

Built with Capacitor. Live camera preview on device; polished fallback backgrounds when the camera is unavailable.

Scan once. Shop smarter for your week.
