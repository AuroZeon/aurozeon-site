Emotional Support, Designed for You

Every feature is built around one idea: no cancer patient should feel alone at night.

Mood Check-In

Record how you feel right now. Optional pain level for doctor visits. Gentle prompts that understand the moment.

AI Heart Companion

Gentle Companion uses secure cloud AI—thoughtful replies with metaphor and warmth, avoids clinical language. Never judges. See our Privacy Policy for how messages are handled.

Symptom Tracker

Daily fatigue, nausea, pain, sleep, appetite. Smart alerts: 3 high-pain days? We suggest questions to prepare for your doctor.

Questions for Your Doctor

Write down questions before visits. Never forget what you wanted to ask at your next checkup.

Treatment Countdown

Set your next treatment or follow-up date. Stay oriented and prepared.

Breathing Exercises

Calm your mind and body. Auto calm mode when you're feeling angry or overwhelmed.
